Problem

People are constantly distracted from important tasks or moments, especially when working from home. So, tasks are not accomplished in time.

According to a survey based on over 2300 respondents in 2020 June, using social media (61%) and smartphones (53%) are ranked as two of the highest distractions for working-from-home professionals. Binge-watching is reported as the third, with 42% of respondents. A survey from Glassdoor also indicates similar concerns for distractions from TV.

    Takeaway

    Nudging people into performing an ideal behavior is also a kind of user-centered design.

    My greatest takeaway from the project is the process of digging up psychological theories and behavior economics and intertwining them into the design. I feel a sense of achievement by knowing that this design is based on solid science and it will very possibly make a positive impact on users.
