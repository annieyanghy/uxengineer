It's a story of finding my greatest passion.

Hi I'm Hsin-yu!


Previously Product Design Intern @ Duo Security / Cisco.

I am a Product Designer who loves humanizing complicated inhumane problems and building rapport with users.


My diverse background in Foreign Languages and Literatures shapes my acuity in storytelling and interests in digging out users' emotion and story.


My endeavour to solve problems has led me to start a sustainable diner and pursuing mindfulness practice and yoga in India in the past. Now, my curiosity of the world and ambition to make an impactful design has led me here at the University of Michigan.

Why UX? –– UX chose me.

Chapter 1

Inquisitive mind for human story

I love literature from all over the world and learning languages to know different cultures has been my passion, which is why I studied Foreign Languages and Literatures and spent 1 year in Germany as an exchange student to polish my German.



Chapter 2

Sprouting love for problem-solving

When I graduated from college and came back from Germany, I wanted to solve the problem of sustainable and ethical eating because I was so inspired by the organic food options and farmers markets in Europe.


Thus, I joined a team of like-minded friends and we started a sustainable vegan restaurant in Taipei.


That's the moment I found I am keen on finding solutions to fix problems and making impacts.



Chapter 3

Sweet spot of Design and Psychology

After leaving the sustainable vegan restaurant, I was trying to figure the greatest passion of my life. I didn't know UX back then, but I know I am fascinated with psychology and design. After endless of trying and exploration, one day I met an article written by a UX designer, who was sharing her thoughts at work.


That was the moment of epiphany. Please allow me to borrow a term from literature.


From the details of the article, I know what she described was exactly what I was looking for –– a perfect combination of design and psychology with an end goal to solve problems for people and make impacts.

About the site

I designed and hand-coded the website from scratch myself. Over several months, I slowly built bits and pieces of the website on a loose schedule during the academic term and speeded up during the winter break.


I simply enjoyed the process of realizing ideas in my head into feasible crafts, which is why I chose to code my portfolio website myself. Hope you also enjoy visiting my website and don't hesitate to reach out for a chat :)
